My Methodology

My teaching methodology is based on interactive and immersive learning. I focus on practical usage of the language, incorporating speaking, listening, reading, and writing exercises in every lesson.

Lessons are designed to be engaging and relevant, using real-life scenarios and materials to make the learning process more relatable and enjoyable. Regular feedback and assessments help track progress and adjust the learning plan as needed.
